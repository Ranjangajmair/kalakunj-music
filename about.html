30 Years of Musical Sadhana, Inspiring Every Note

Nishant Banyat

With an inspiring journey of over 30 years in music sadhana, Nishant Banyat has dedicated his life to the art of music, teaching and inspiring countless students across various genres. Specializing in both Eastern and Western music, he imparts his extensive knowledge through instruction in guitar, sitar, drums, vocals, piano, and flute. Over the years, he has trained thousands of individuals and groups, helping them unlock their musical potential and develop their skills.

Nishant's commitment to creativity and excellence culminated in an extraordinary achievement: his team successfully filmed the highest altitude music video on land at an astonishing elevation of 5,105 meters (16,748 feet 8.2 inches) in Tilicho, Nepal. This remarkable milestone has earned a place in the illustrious pages of the Guinness World Records, symbolizing the perseverance, creativity, and indomitable spirit of human endeavor.

In addition to his teaching, Nishant has served as a judge in various music competitions and has consulted many bands in Nepal, sharing his expertise and fostering the growth of the local music scene. As a mentor and educator, he continues to inspire a new generation of musicians, helping them discover the profound joy of music.
